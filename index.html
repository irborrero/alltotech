All To Tech

Non profit organization helping candidates land SDE offers in Big Tech companies.

How it works

  1. Interview

    Apply for the interview process. Once you send your candidature, you will be contacted by somebody from the program to schedule an interview. We will ask you some questions and do a coding interview. Don't worry! You don't need to prepare for this. We just want to get to know you a bit better.

  2. Training program

    Selected candidates are paired with a mentor inside a Big Tech company. Your mentor will be the person that working with you for the next 2 months! The program will walk you through data structures and algorithms that you need to master in order to pass a coding interview in any tech company. You will also practice doing mock interviews with your mentor.

  3. Referral

    At the end of the program you will be referred for an internship opportunity by your mentor and will be able to test your skills in real world interviews!
