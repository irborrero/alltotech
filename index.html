All To Tech

Non profit organization helping candidates land SDE offers in Big Tech companies.

How it works

  1. Interview

    Apply for the interview process. Once you send your application, you will be contacted by somebody from the organization to schedule an interview. We will ask you some questions and do a coding interview.
    Don't worry! You don't need to prepare for this. We just want to get to know you a bit better.

  2. Training

    Selected candidates will be assigned a mentor inside a Big Tech company. Your mentor will be the person working with you for the next 2 months.
    The program will walk you through data structures and algorithms that you need to master in order to face technical interviews. You will also practice by doing mock interviews and other group activities.

  3. Referral

    At the end of the program you will be referred for an internship opportunity by your mentor and will be able to test your skills in real world interviews!
